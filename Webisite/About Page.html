About Talent Shepherd

Your trusted partner in global relocation and immigration services

Who Are We

Talent Shepherd is an agency committed to simplifying and accelerating the immigration journey for individuals and families seeking to relocate abroad. We provide end-to-end solutions from consultations to post-arrival support ensuring a seamless transition to your dream destination. Our expert advisors offer personalized guidance on visa processes for work, study, investment, or family reunification, with a 90%+ approval rate. We specialize in immigration to Gulf countries, Schengen countries, the UK, and Turkey, providing region-specific expertise. Beyond applications, we assist with job placements, scholarships, and credential evaluations to unlock global opportunities. Our settlement services cover housing, cultural orientation, and local integration to help you start your new life confidently. At Talent Shepherd, we combine efficiency with empathy, turning complex processes into achievable milestones because, we’re not just moving people, we’re Building Teams, Shaping Success, empowering you to thrive beyond borders.

Our Mission

Our History

2010 - Foundation

Talent Shepherd was founded in Nairobi by immigration experts who saw the need for personalized relocation services in East Africa.

2013 - First Major Success

We helped our 97th client secure a Newziland work visa, marking our breakthrough in the industry.

2016 - Expansion

Opened other branches around East Africa to assist more people to access great global opportunities.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Launched our fully digital application tracking system, reducing processing times by 30%.

2023 - Milestone Achivement

Reached 900 successful relocations with a 90% approval rate across all visa categories.

2025 - Today

Hoping to Expand our services to 15+ countries, maintaining a 92% approval rate and pioneering AI-driven visa consultancy
