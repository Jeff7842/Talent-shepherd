Germany Immigration Programs

Canada offers multiple pathways to permanent residency and citizenship. Choose the program that best fits your qualifications and goals.

Express Entry

Fast-track immigration for skilled workers with points-based selection.

  • Processing time: 6-8 months
  • CRS score requirement: 470+
  • Language proficiency required

Study Permit

Pathway to permanent residency through Canadian education.

  • Post-graduation work permit available

  • Pathway to PR after graduation

Provincial Nominee

  • Each province has unique criteria
  • Faster processing than federal programs
  • Job offer often required

Family Sponsorship

Canada offers universal healthcare, excellent public services, and a high standard of living. Canadians enjoy clean air, safe communities, and abundant natural spaces.

Cost of Living

While costs vary by city, Canada offers affordable living compared to other developed nations. Expect to spend:

  • $1,500-$3,000/month for rent (2-bedroom apartment)
  • $200-$400/month for groceries (family of 4)
  • $100-$150/month for public transit

Our Visa Application Process

01

Consultation:

We begin with a detailed consultation to understand your travel plans and visa requirements. During this consultation, we assess your eligibility for the Schengen visa and provide guidance on the necessary documentation.

02

Document Preparation:

Our team assists you in gathering and preparing all required documents, including application forms, travel itineraries, and financial statements. We ensure your application meets the specific requirements of the Schengen member countries.

03

Application Submission:

Once the documents are ready, we submit your visa application to the designated Schengen visa application center on your behalf. We track the progress of your application and provide updates as necessary.

04

Follow-Up:

We follow up on your application's progress and address any additional requests or queries from the visa authorities. We provide timely updates and support throughout the process.

05

Visa Collection:

Once your visa is approved, we assist you with the collection process and provide guidance on next steps for your travel plans.
